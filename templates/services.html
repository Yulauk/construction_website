Step: 1

{{ _('Consultation') }}

I take pride in providing a high level of personalized service, starting from the first day in your home. We aim to understand your living space and you better. We want to learn about your ideas and project vision, as well as address any specific needs. We welcome any photos or sketches you've created. Incorporating the client's personal preferences into the design allows us to create a unique and functional space. We also keep an eye on the latest trends in design and construction, offering innovative solutions.

In our approach, we implement project management systems for transparency, efficiency, and adherence to deadlines. Demonstrating an interest in sustainable design and using environmentally friendly materials is an integral part of our philosophy. Personal involvement in the project and a willingness to work closely with clients ensure the successful realization of their dreams. Within seven days, we will provide you with a preliminary proposal, including an estimated cost breakdown.

{{ _('COST: Free') }}

Step: 2

{{ _('Design') }}

After the approval of the estimate, we move on to the design and preparation phase for construction. Our architect-designers will strive to align your project program with your construction budget. At this stage, we explore and select materials, as well as create conceptual, approval, and construction drawings. We also generate three-dimensional images to help you visualize the new space. This critical stage of the project typically takes more time than the actual construction and requires close coordination with your input as the client. We streamline the process by creating a detailed design schedule outlining tasks, responsible parties, and anticipated stages.

{{ _("COST: typically 10 – 15%% of estimated construction cost") }}

Step: 3

{{ _('Construction') }}

Once the project is complete, and you've signed the construction contract, we will obtain permits and commence work on-site. Readiness to address unforeseen challenges or make adjustments in the event of changing circumstances is a crucial aspect of our approach. In case unexpected situations arise, we take measures to minimize potential delays, ensuring the smooth progress of the project.

Specific stages will be implemented on the construction site every week or month, and we will provide you with a detailed schedule so that you are always aware of our progress.

As a client, you have the opportunity to provide your comments or suggestions during the construction process. We are open to interaction through regular meetings, online platforms, or other communication tools. Your feedback is essential to us, and we strive to ensure effective communication by providing regular updates and promptly addressing questions or concerns.

We offer 24/7 access via phone and email, even on weekends, to promptly respond to any questions or changes that may arise during construction. Your construction-stage coordinator will keep you informed daily about our progress and is ready to answer all your inquiries.

{{ _('COST: by agreement') }}

Step: 4

{{ _('Closeout') }}

When our team completes the construction:

After a thorough cleanup of the construction site, we will conduct a final review with you to ensure that every detail meets your expectations. We value your feedback, and your input in the form of an evaluation will be a crucial component of our commitment to continuous service improvement.

If, after completing the main stages of the project, you wish to add additional finishing works, we are ready to provide additional services. Your satisfaction is our top priority.

Upon project completion, we will stay in touch, ready to address any questions or issues that may arise after inhabiting the renovated space. Our team is prepared to offer additional services if future support is needed.

In the event of any suggestions or desires for changes, we are open to personal discussions and adjustments to ensure that your home fully meets all your requirements. Your comfort and satisfaction are our primary concerns.
