{{ _(' Rough construction works in the building industry represent the initial stage of creating a building or structure. This stage includes the execution of fundamental construction and engineering activities to ensure strength, stability, and readiness for subsequent finishing stages. Here is a comprehensive description of rough construction works:

1. Groundwork:
  • Leveling and clearing the site from vegetation and foreign objects.
  • Installing drainage systems if necessary.
  • Compacting the soil and creating a preparatory base for the foundation.
2. Foundation Works:
  • Constructing foundation walls and/or strip foundations according to the project documentation.
  • Installing reinforcement and pouring concrete.
3. Construction of Load-Bearing Walls:
  • Erecting load-bearing walls from brick, concrete, blocks, or other materials.
  • Installing necessary steel elements for rigidity and strength.
4. Installation of Floor Structures:
  • Placing concrete slabs, metal beams, or other structures to create floors between levels.
5. Roofing Works:
  • Constructing the roof framework.
  • Installing the roof truss system and laying roofing materials.
6. Engineering Systems:
  • Installing water supply and sewage systems.
  • Mounting electrical wiring, heating, and ventilation systems.
7. Facade Finishing:
  • Finishing external walls with materials providing thermal insulation and protection against atmospheric influences.
8. Final Interior Finishing Works:
  • Smoothing walls and ceilings.
  • Preparing the floor for subsequent finishing.
9. Preparation for Networking Equipment:
  • Laying routes for electrical wiring, telephone, and internet cables.
10. Inspection and Quality Control:
  • Conducting inspections to identify possible defects and discrepancies from the project documentation.
Rough construction works create the foundation for further finishing works, ensuring the stability and functionality of the building. This stage is the cornerstone for the successful completion of a construction project. ') }}
