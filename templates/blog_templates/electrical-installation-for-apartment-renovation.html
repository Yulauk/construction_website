{{ _(' Electrical installation work during a major apartment renovation includes the following stages:

1. Preliminary work:
  • Assessment of the condition of electrical wiring and equipment.
  • Development of an electrical installation project, taking into account modern standards and safety requirements.
  • Obtaining necessary permits and approvals for the work if required.
2. Installation of the distribution board and wiring:
  • Installation of a new or upgrade of an existing distribution board.
  • Routing new electrical wiring along walls and ceilings, considering the apartment layout and the placement of outlets, switches, and light fixtures.
3. Installation of outlets, switches, and light fixtures:
  • Installation of outlets and switches according to the project.
  • Connection of lighting fixtures and lamps.
4. Installation of grounding system:
  • Installation of grounding wire along the walls and its connection to the grounding device or ground bus.
5. Installation of protection against electric shock:
  • Installation of differential circuit breakers (RCDs) or differential current relays (DCRs) to provide protection against electric shock.
6. Connection of electrical appliances and systems:
  • Connection of kitchen and household appliances, water heaters, air conditioners, and other electrical appliances.
7. Testing and system setup:
  • Conducting tests on electrical equipment to ensure compliance with safety standards.
  • Calibration and verification of the electrical installations.
8. Documentation and completion of work:
  • Compilation of a acceptance report for the electrical installation work by the client.
  • Providing technical documentation and warranty commitments for the completed work.
It is crucial to adhere to all safety requirements when performing electrical installation work and trust the execution of such tasks to experienced professionals. ') }}
