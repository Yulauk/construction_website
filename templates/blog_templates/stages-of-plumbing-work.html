{{ _(" Stages of Plumbing Works in Comprehensive Apartment Renovation

1. Preliminary work:
  • Assessment of the existing plumbing and utilities.
  • Development of a plumbing project considering the apartment layout and client's requirements.
  • If necessary, obtaining permits and approvals for the works.
2. Demolition of Existing Plumbing:
  • Dismantling old plumbing fixtures (baths, toilets, sinks, shower cabins, etc.).
  • Removal of old pipes and utilities.
3. Installation of New Pipes and Utilities:
  • Lay new water supply and sewage pipes according to the project.
  • Installation of drainage cisterns, valves, and other elements of the sewage system.
4. Installation of Plumbing Equipment:
  • Installing new plumbing fixtures (baths, toilets, sinks, shower cabins, etc.).
  • Connecting plumbing devices to water supply and sewage pipes.
5. Heating System Installation:
  • Installing heating radiators.
  • Laying heating pipes and connecting them to the boiler or another heat source.
6. Installation of Ventilation and Air Conditioning Systems:
  • Installing ventilation grilles and exhaust fans.
  • Laying ventilation ducts and connecting them to the overall house ventilation system.
7. Connection of Kitchen and Household Appliances:
  • Connecting the kitchen sink, dishwasher, washing machine, and other household appliances to water supply and sewage systems.
8. Conducting Tests and System Calibration:
  • Checking the tightness of plumbing systems.
  • Calibration and verification of the functioning of plumbing equipment.
9. Documentation and Completion of Works:
  • Compilation of an acceptance report by the client for the plumbing works.
  • Providing technical documentation and warranty commitments for the completed works.
Each stage demands careful supervision and high-quality execution to ensure the reliable and safe operation of the apartment's plumbing systems. ") }}
